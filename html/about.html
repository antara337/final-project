About me

My name is Antara and I'm a freshman at New Jersey Institute of Technology. I'm majoring in Human-Computer Interaction, and I hope to become a UX designer and developer after graduating. I picked this major because I felt it evenly balanced out my technical abilities and design interests and skills.
I've always had a passion for video editing, and I began self teaching myself in August of 2017. A lot has changed in the past 6 years, and editing has helped me explore and learn who I am through my passions. Connecting to all different parts of the world, I've gained a significant amount of perspective and I wouldn't be who I am today without it.
